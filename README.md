🍽️ UniEats – Campus Food Pre-Order & Pickup System
📌 Project Overview

UniEats is a web-based campus food pre-order and pickup system designed to reduce long queues at university cafeterias.
The system allows students to pre-order food from multiple campus vendors and collect it at scheduled pickup times, improving time management and overall campus efficiency.

This project is developed as part of IT3040 – IT Project Management (ITPM).

🎯 Objectives

Reduce waiting time at campus food outlets

Improve student time management during academic hours

Provide a simple and efficient food pre-ordering process

Enable vendors to manage orders and food availability effectively

👥 Target Users

University students

Campus food vendors (cafeterias, juice bars, snack shops)

🧩 System Modules

The system is divided into four main components, each owned by a team member:

Student Profile, Preferences & Feedback Module

Student registration using university email

Dietary preferences and order history

Food and vendor feedback

Food Menu & Vendor Display Module

Display of campus vendors

Food items with price and availability

Search and filter options

Order & Pickup Scheduling Module

Cart management

Pickup time slot selection

Order creation and status tracking

Vendor Order Management Dashboard

Vendor login with role-based access

View and manage incoming orders

Update order status

Manage food availability

View daily order summaries

🛠️ Project Scope

Web-based application

Supports pre-order and on-site pickup only

No online payment or delivery functionality

Designed for completion within an 11-week timeframe

Focused on campus-based usage
